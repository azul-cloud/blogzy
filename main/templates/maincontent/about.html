About Travel Blog Wave

What is Travel Blog Wave?

Travel Blog Wave was founded by a nomadic web developer/blogger looking to improve the world of travel blogging. Travel blogs have been gaining traction in recent years, but there still is so much room to grow. The goal of Travel Blog Wave is to create the best travel blogging platform out there, for both readers and writers. Instead of just creating the an amazing travel blog, why not create an amazing travel blog platform for everyone to enjoy?

We plan to be highly influenced by our users and keep the functionality of the site at the highest level possible. We're committed to providing the best resource on the market and are open to any suggestions for improvement!
